# PLF-014: Parse LinuxFr poll pages

Backlog tickets may be incomplete. Before moving this ticket to `planned`, refine whether polls are valuable enough for the next implementation cycle.

## Objective

Parse collected LinuxFr poll pages into structured poll data that can support Poll vs Praxis and historical community self-image analyses.

## Context

Several use-case challenge responses proposed comparing polls with lived discussion, predictions, or community self-image. The expanded radar run collected poll seeds but did not find poll details useful for that topic. Poll support is therefore promising but not urgent.

Motivating sources:

- `docs/use-case-challenge-prompts/bold-use-cases/response-glm-zai.md`
- `docs/use-case-challenge-prompts/bold-use-cases/response-grok.md`
- `docs/use-case-challenge-prompts/bold-use-cases/response-claude.md`
- `docs/prototype-prompts/agentic-ai-career-radar-expanded/review.md`

## Scope

In scope:

- Parse already collected public LinuxFr poll pages.
- Extract question, options, counts or percentages when visible, date, source URL, and local raw path.
- Produce a small structured representation suitable for wiki notes or reports.

Out of scope:

- Voting or authenticated actions.
- Poll discovery or crawling.
- Statistical claims beyond visible poll data.
- Comment collection around polls unless handled by comment tickets.
